Why This Module Is Essential

The finance module is directly connected to operations. Each scheduled game can generate projected and actual transportation costs, rink costs, referee costs, ticket sales, and merchandise revenue. At the same time, the system also tracks non-game team finances such as jersey purchases, glove orders, sponsorships, and donations. This gives the administrator a complete and simple financial overview.
